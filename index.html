Who Is Patrick Kennelly?

Industry Expert

Patrick Kennelly is a seasoned electrical industry professional and the proud owner of Anthony G. Ferry Inc., a company built on trust, craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to delivering reliable electrical solutions. With over two decades of hands-on experience, Patrick has become a trusted name in the field — known for his technical mastery, ethical standards, and dedication to his clients.

Patrick's journey in the electrical industry began with a deep curiosity for how things work and a passion for building systems that last. Over the years, he has led complex residential, commercial, and industrial projects, earning a reputation as someone who not only understands the technical demands of the trade but also prioritizes the safety and satisfaction of every client he serves.

As featured in Fox Interview, Nairaland, and YouTube, Patrick is recognized for his thought leadership and his drive to elevate industry standards through innovation and responsibility.
